Wimbledon champion Marketa Vondrousova lost after being ahead 6-2, 5-1 against the 22nd-ranked Sorana Cirstea in the Dubai Championships quarterfinals on Thursday. Vondrousova blew six match points as Cirstea won 2-6, 7-6 (1), 6-2. World No. 3 Coco Gauff also lost from a set up when she was run down by qualifier Anna Kalinskaya 2-6, 6-4, 6-2. That left No. 1 Iga Swiatek as the only seed in the semifinals from 16 that started the tournament. Swiatek will play 40th-ranked Anna Kalinskaya on Friday. The 22nd-ranked Sorana Cirstea will be facing 26th-ranked Jasmine Paolini of Italy in the other semi.
