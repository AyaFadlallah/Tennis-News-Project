Just as the verdict of Simona Halep’s appeal of her doping ban was revealed, so too was the announcement of her return to tennis. This week at the Miami Open, the two-time Grand Slam champion will return to action for the first time since the 2022 US Open. She will play her first round against former No. 2 Paula Badosa, with the winner facing Aryna Sabalenka. You can thank James Blake for that. “That one’s on me,” the Miami tournament director said on Served with Roddick. “When it comes to some of the tennis decisions they're going to trust me.”
