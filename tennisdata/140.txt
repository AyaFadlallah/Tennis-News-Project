With last year’s winner Petra Kvitova out on maternity leave, there will be a new women’s champion crowned at the Miami Open. But will it be one of the pre-tournament favorites and top seeds, or will one of the many looming threats and dangerous sleepers throw a wrench in the works? An unseeded Naomi Osaka—who reached the final here in 2022—was drawn into No. 3 seed Coco Gauff’s section, and they could meet in the fourth round. Then there’s the ultimate wild card—a returning Simona Halep, back with a vengeance after CAS’s ruling ended her doping suspension.
