Fast-rising Mirra Andreeva took an “unforgettable” detour on her way home from Indian Wells, as she recently stopped by the Nike World Headquarters in Beaverton, Ore. for a day with the sports apparel giant. The 16-year-old posted up a storm on social media to document her trip, and shared a video of her experience on Instagram. “Visited Nike’s home!!” Andreeva wrote in a caption. “What an awesome journey it was… Thank you all for this unforgettable day.” Andreeva seemed to enjoy every minute of her trip to the campus, which includes buildings named after the brand’s biggest stars Michael Jordan.
