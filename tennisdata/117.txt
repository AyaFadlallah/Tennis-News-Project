This kind of clarity makes it easier to understand how Swiatek has been able to bear up under the stress. I admit to having been somewhat puzzled when Swiatek first appeared on the scene and caught the eye of the pundit class. Sure, her spidery athleticism was obvious, but that conventional-looking game, while solid, didn’t seem to be anything more than that. Besides, in roughly that time frame we had witnessed a remarkable succession of intriguing emergences: Marketa Vondrousova, Sofia Kenin, Barbora Krejcikova.. Most of them were, very good—but not great. Swiatek has become great, with an upper-case “G.”
