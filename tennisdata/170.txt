On Wednesday, Coco Gauff ushered in her 20s with an emphatic 6-0, 6-2 victory over 24th seed Elise Mertens to reach the BNP Paribas Open quarterfinals for the second year running. Afterwards, she was equally assertive with putting her cravings to bed. Speaking with Steve Weissman and Prakash Amritraj on Tennis Channel, Coco Gauff was presented with her first 20th birthday cake. Keeping in line with tradition, she made a wish while blowing out candles, but what followed took the hosts and everyone on set by surprise in the best way possible. “You want to show the people?” Weissman asked.
