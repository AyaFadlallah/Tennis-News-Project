With the likes of Naomi Osaka, Elina Svitolina and Victoria Azarenka all headlining the Miami Open women’s draw, it’s been all about the WTA moms during the second half of the Sunshine Swing. But let’s give it up for some of the ATP dads, too—or at least, the wives of ATP dads. Ahead of his debut at Hard Rock Stadium, defending champion Daniil Medvedev heaped praise on his wife Daria as he opened up about work-life balance and fatherhood on the road. “As a player, I don’t think (fatherhood) has changed me, to be honest,” Medvedev told the press.
