 Aryna Sabalenka lost her first match since successfully defending her Australian Open title when she was beaten by an old foe at the Dubai Championships on Tuesday. The No. 2-ranked Sabalenka blew a set and a 2-0 lead as Donna Vekic of Croatia rallied to win 6-7 (5), 6-3, 6-0 in the second round. Iga Swiatek lost her serve three times in the first set before coming right and beating Sloane Stephens 6-4, 6-4. Coco Gauff, the No. 3, accounted for Elisabetta Cocciaretto of Italy 6-1, 7-5. No. 4 Elena Rybakina avoided an upset when Victoria Azarenka retired in distress.
