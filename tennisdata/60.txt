World No. 2 Carlos Alcaraz lost in the quarterfinals of the Australian Open, going down to No. 6 seed Alexander Zverev in four enthralling sets, 6-1, 6-3, 6-7(2), 6-4. Carlos Alcaraz had already achieved a career-best result in Melbourne located in Australia coming into the quarterfinal matchup, but Alexander Zverev jumped out early to take the first two sets. World No. 2 fought back in the third -- breaking Alexander Zverev who was serving for the match at 5-3 -- but was unable to sustain his level in the fourth, bowing out in Melbourne in three hours and six minutes.
