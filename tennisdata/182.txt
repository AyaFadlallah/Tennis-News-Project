A strong start to the new season has Casper Ruud feeling as light as his newly trimmed physique at the BNP Paribas Open. “Last year in February I was around 84 kilos and this year I'm 80, 4 less!” Ruud explained during his post-match press conference. “Maybe doesn't sound too much, but it's 5% of my weight, which is something.” Ruud famously spent last season a step slower than his competitors after a 2022 exhibition tour alongside Rafael Nadal left him without a proper off-season and caused him to tumble outside the Top 10 from a career-high of No. 2.
