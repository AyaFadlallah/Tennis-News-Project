Now it appears Nick Kyrgios is set to announce the next phase of his comeback plan, with one of the most colorful characters in the game posting a cryptic message on his X account to pre-empt his big news. Created by Patrick Mouratoglou, the legendary coach of Serena Williams, UTS is a fast-paced, explosive version of tennis that has proved to be a big hit with fans around the world in recent years.
