Paula Badosa admits Aryna Sabalenka is going through a difficult time following the death of her partner Konstantin Koltsov, but has promised her friend “not to talk about” the situation in public. The tennis community was left in shock on Tuesday after it was confirmed that former Belarus international and NHL ice hockey player Konstantin Koltsov had passed away at the age of 42 in Miami. Despite the tragic news, the reigning Australian Open winner’s team have confirmed that Aryna Sabalenka intends to play at this week’s WTA 1000 event and she was seen practicing at the event on Tuesday.
