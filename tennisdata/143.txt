“Just like any sport, tennis finds itself with many fan bases–ranging from casual and lapsed fans to hardcore and gambling fans,” says David Carter, principal of The Sports Group and adjunct professor of sports business at USC. “They each require a different tech experience so those focusing on tech development must always appreciate this. Ultimately, fans will dictate the type and pace of tech development and, if properly deployed, a governing body or tournament can drive incremental revenue from media partners, sponsors, and fans if they thread this needle properly.” Fans who come to the tournament are seeking experiences.
