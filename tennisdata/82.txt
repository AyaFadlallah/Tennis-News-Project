Ivo Karlovic finished his 25-year career with eight singles titles and a record of 371 wins, 346 losses, and $10.1 million in prize money. His highest singles ranking was at No. 14 in August 2008. His best Grand Slam singles performance was at Wimbledon in 2009, when he advanced to the quarterfinals. He also won the Davis Cup in 2005 with Croatia when it beat Slovakia in the final for the country's first title. Six-time Grand Slam singles champion Boris Becker was among those to congratulate the towering Croatian Ivo Karlovic on X, calling his serve "the best in tennis."
