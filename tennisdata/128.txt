Business acumen has been important to Chris Eubanks since his days as a business administration major on the Georgia Tech University men's tennis team, and now, the American is using that status as a touring tennis pro to impart that knowledge on others. The 27-year-old American Chris Eubanks recently inked a sponsorship deal with FICO, the software analytics firm responsible for the FICO score—used by 90% of U.S. banks and loan lenders to track a person's credit and determine their eligibility for personal loans, mortgages, auto loans, credit cards, and more—in the hopes of building Americans' financial literacy.
