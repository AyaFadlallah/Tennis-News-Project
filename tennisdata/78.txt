Novak Djokovic is set to return to the Miami Open next month after a five-year hiatus because of COVID-19 and U.S. travel restrictions after being included on the entry list released by tournament organizers. Reigning champion Daniil Medvedev and 2023 finalist Jannik Sinner are also on the entry list, with the latter having since avenged that loss when he beat the Russian in the Australian Open final to become the first Italian man to win a Grand Slam title since 1976. Among the other names in the men's field are  Alcaraz, Taylor Fritz, Tommy Paul, Frances Tiafoe and Ben Shelton.
