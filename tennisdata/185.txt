Daria Kasatkina has grown more comfortable in front of the camera the last 18 months as the popularity of her and Natalia Zabiiako's joint behind-the-scenes vlog that tells the story of the tennis tour has exploded. And on Hollywood's biggest night, she was making her cinematic opinions known as she delivered live commentary of the Oscars on X, formerly Twitter. The Russian flashed her stan card in support of friend-of-tennis Emma Stone—who famously portrayed Billie Jean King in the "Battle of the Sexes" film —who took home the top prize of Best Actress for her performance in "Poor Things."
