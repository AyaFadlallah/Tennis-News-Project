Coco Gauff’s coach Brad Gilbert has revealed he was bombarded with texts calling for him to “fix” the American star’s forehand and grip after he started working with her.  The coaching great asserted it is more difficult for players to make technical changes than many realise as he declared “not everybody” can frequently make adjustments like Rafael Nadal or Andre Agassi. Brad Gilbert, who coached American legends Agassi and Andy Roddick to major titles and the world No 1 ranking, has helped Coco Gauff reach new heights since joining her team ahead of the Citi Open in Washington last year.
