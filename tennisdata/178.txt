
Reports that the Netflix docuseries Break Point was unlikely to return for a third season didn’t come as a surprise to the players and former cast members competing at the BNP Paribas Open. The camera crew that had trailed a cadre of the ATP and WTA tour’s best and brightest for the last two years has been conspicuously absent since the 2024 tennis season began in January. “I enjoyed filming with them,” said Aryna Sabalenka, whose rise from contender to champion was captured over both seasons. “I think they did a great job. But, yeah, I will miss the crew.”
