Racquet Preview: Coco Gauff's new frame, the Head Boom 2024. The second generation of the user-friendly frame gets updated for greater comfort and stability. Being just the second generation of the franchise, The Boom is Head’s crowd-pleasing franchise. It’s a forgiving and versatile racquet that can suit a wide range of players and styles. Its unique head shape is wider in the upper hoop to expand the sweet spot for easy power, generous spin production, and clean hitting. It’s the type of frame that feels good in hand and even better at contact, encouraging users to go for their shots.
