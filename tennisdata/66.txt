DUBAI, United Arab Emirates -- Jasmine Paolini earned the biggest victory of her career by defeating qualifier Anna Kalinskaya 4-6, 7-5, 7-5 to win the Dubai Championships title on Saturday. The 26th-ranked Paolini fought back from a breakdown in both the second and third sets en route to her second career WTA singles title and first at the 1000 level. Paolini, 28, avenged her loss to Kalinskaya from a month ago in the Australian Open fourth round, and Saturday's victory assures her of a top-20 debut when the rankings are updated Monday.
