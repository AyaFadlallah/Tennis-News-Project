 Holger Rune eased past Michael Mmoh 6-2, 6-3 and advanced to the second-round. Frances Tiafoe, seeded eighth, rallied to beat Max Purcell 4-6, 6-3, 6-0. He moved on to face Dominik Koepfer, who took care of Terence Atmane 6-4, 4-6, 6-0. Tiafoe is the highest-ranked American player in contention in the tournament after fourth-seeded Taylor Fritz and seventh-seeded Tommy Paul were knocked out on Monday. In another result, Casper Ruud outlasted Christopher Eubanks 6-4, 6-7 (5), 6-3 and moved on to the second round. Ruud is going to play against Dusan Lajovic, who beat local Rodrigo Pacheco Mendez 6-2, 6-2.
