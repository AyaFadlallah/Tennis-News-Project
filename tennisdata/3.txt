Simona Halep has hit back at Caroline Wozniacki following the Dane’s controversial comments on the back of Miami Open organisers’ decision to hand Halep a wildcard for the tournament. Playing for the first time since August 2022 after her doping suspension was reduced from four years to nine months, Halep produced a solid effort against Paula Badosa in the first round on Tuesday but eventually went down 1-6, 6-4, 6-3.
