Let’s not read too much into the 123rd-ranked lucky loser’s upset of the all-time men’s Grand Slam singles champion. Novak Djokovic found himself in the wrong place at the wrong time in the Indian Wells Tennis Garden on a windy, cool Monday evening. It happens. Still, this astonishing result points to some teachable moments about the game, starting with the advantage Nardi mentioned: the ability to have closely watched so many of Djokovic’s matches in our media-rich era.
